Account

Delete your Matheasy account

You may permanently delete your Matheasy account and associated data at any time. This page explains exactly how.

Delete in the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside Matheasy. Follow these steps:

1
Open Matheasy
2
Sign in to your account
3
Go to your Profile
4
Open Settings
5
Tap Delete Account
6
Confirm deletion

Prefer to delete by email?

If you can't access the app, send us a deletion request. Email the address below from the account you'd like to delete, using the subject line "Delete My Account". We'll process your request within 48 hours.

support@getmatheasy.com
Subject: Delete My Account

Deleting your account is permanent

Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be reversed. Please make sure before you confirm. When you delete your account:

  • Your account information will be permanently removed.
  • Your learning history and saved solutions may be deleted.
  • Any subscription benefits associated with the account may be lost.
  • This action cannot be reversed or recovered.
A note on subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel a paid subscription. To stop future charges, also cancel your subscription in your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings. See our Support center for step-by-step help.

We're happy to help you with any account concern.
